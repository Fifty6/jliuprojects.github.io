CLIENTS ABOUT PRESS SHOP

Tell us about yourself! How old are you? Where are you from? What's your story?

#HelloMyNameIs Elie Kimbembe (@visionelie). I'm 21 year old artist specializing in photography and design from Toronto. I was born in Congo and moved to Canada when I was a child, so travelling has always been something that's been influencing my life, and thus, my photography. I've been taking photography seriously for the last year and a half and have been lucky enough to work with some great creatives all over the world.

What is your favorite thing to photograph?

I don't have a particular subject that I would classify as my favourite thing to photograph just because my style is everything and everyone in my environment. I guess if there's one theme to my photography it would be the culture I've immersed myself in. I typically put myself in situations with like-minded creatives from Toronto and go from there. It's weird, but I've somehow found ways to always find good photo opportunities everywhere I go - whether it's cool architecture or my good looking friends.

What about your life do you share on your Instagram feed?

I'm young so I'm constantly trying to get it. I like to show the people and things I admire most.

What do you choose to leave out?

I only share a small amount of my personal life on Instagram. My feed isn't a real reflection of my daily life. It's ironic, but the more I've been building on Instagram the more private I've become.

What would you like people to know about you?

Although I've been going hard with photography, I'm trying to work my way up to start directing film pieces. I've partnered with Kid. Studio in Toronto - a design & film studio. They're really helping me learn and grow my skills in both those areas.
